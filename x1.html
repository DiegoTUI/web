The Stuart Hotel - Derby

4Self Classified Hotel Ratings explained
119 London Road , Derby, DE1 2QR
SPECIAL OFFERS OF UP TO 20% OFF ROOM BOOKINGS BY PURCHASING IN ADVANCE. SEE OUR SPECIAL OFFERS PAGE.*
The Stuart Hotel is in Derby City Centre, walking distance from Midland Rail Station, Westfield Shopping Centre, many restaurants, bars and the theatre. The hotel is modern and contemporary, stylishly designed to offer a comfortable environment with excellent standards of service and customer care. You will enjoy the hip Liquid Bar and international cuisine freshly prepared on-site for our XS Restaurant.

Guests have access to FREE WIFI as well as comfortable modern rooms with great facilities. There is ample FREE ON-SITE CAR PARKING, the hotel is ideal for the business or leisure traveller wanting a relaxing and comfortable stay.All rooms are non-smoking.
